Get the Styled Experience

Styled is a personal style consultacy based in Cork. Our mission is to help people shop with confidence for their style and their budget. We specialise in 1:1 consultations for personal shopping, image consultations and wardrobe refreshes. How you dress can have a true impact on your confidence. We aim to personalise the hugely impersonal online shopping world, enabling you to make inspired fashion choices, spend your time and money wisely and end up looking and feeling amazing.
